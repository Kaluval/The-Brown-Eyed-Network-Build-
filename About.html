Our story


​At B.E.N, we are on a mission to redefine the African music landscape by empowering musicians across the continent. Founded by Valentine Kalu, a visionary leader with a background in software engineering and a deep love for music, B.E.N emerged from a desire to address the challenges faced by artists. Inspired by a bath-time revelation (yes, seriously!), Valentine envisioned a platform that not only supports the creative process but also ensures global exposure and collaboration opportunities.
